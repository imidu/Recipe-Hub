Appetizer Recipes

1. Bruschetta

Ingredients:

  • Baguette slices
  • Tomatoes
  • Basil
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic

Steps:

  1. Toast the baguette slices.
  2. Rub with garlic and drizzle with olive oil.
  3. Top with diced tomatoes and basil.

2. Stuffed Mushrooms

Ingredients:

  • Button mushrooms
  • Cream cheese
  • Bread crumbs
  • Parsley
  • Salt & pepper

Steps:

  1. Clean mushrooms and remove stems.
  2. Mix cream cheese, bread crumbs, parsley, salt, and pepper.
